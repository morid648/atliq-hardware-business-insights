# Presentation – Atliq Hardware Business Insights

## Overview

This presentation summarizes the key findings from the SQL-based analysis of Atliq Hardware’s business data.

It is designed for **top-level management** to support strategic decision-making.

---

## What This Presentation Covers

• Business problem and objectives  
• Database overview  
• Product portfolio growth  
• Product segment analysis  
• Sales channel contribution  
• Customer discount analysis  
• Key insights and recommendations  

---

## Key Highlights

• Product portfolio grew by **36% (2020 → 2021)**  
• Notebook and accessories dominate product segments  
• Retail channel contributes **73% of total revenue**  
• Q4 recorded the highest sales performance  
• Major customers receive ~30% discounts  

---

## Purpose

The goal of this presentation is to:

• Convert SQL analysis into business insights  
• Help management make data-driven decisions  
• Highlight growth opportunities and risks
